About Dillon

Hi, I'm Dillon. My mission is to help teams develop and implement strategies using data-driven solutions to attain desired outcomes. I specialize in Front-End Web Design and Development, Data Analysis, Outcomes Research, and Quality Improvement. I am a 2014 graduate of the Medical University of South Carolina Masters in Health Administration program, and a 2011 graduate of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with a Bachelors Degree in Economics. I am a Certified Associate in Healthcare Information and Management Systems through HIMSS. I have completed the CODEcamp Web Basics Series through the Charleston Digital Corridor, as well as many online programming courses. I am active in the tech community, participating in organizations such as the Charleston Technology Group and the Charleston Data Analytics Group. I work well in both team-oriented and self-directed environments. I welcome the opportunity to connect.
